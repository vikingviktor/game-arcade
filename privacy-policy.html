Privacy Policy for Game Arcade

Last Updated: December 26, 2025

Overview

Game Arcade ("Top War" and "Ski Slope") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information Collection

Our app does not collect, store, or share any personal information. We do not:

  • Collect your name, email, or contact information
  • Track your location
  • Access your device's camera, microphone, or contacts
  • Store any gameplay data on external servers
  • Use analytics or tracking services

Data Storage

All game progress and settings are stored locally on your device only. This data includes:

  • Game scores and progress
  • User preferences and settings

This information never leaves your device and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

Our app does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect information from children or anyone else.

Security

Since we don't collect or transmit any personal data, there are no security risks associated with data breaches or unauthorized access.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
